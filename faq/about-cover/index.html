About Cover

Hey user! Welcome to Cover. We want to take the time to let you know everything there is to know about Cover. We’ve worked hard to develop this content vault app. It is the first of its kind private photo and video vault which was created by creating the state of the art machine learning processes. Cover is able to automatically detect and store any incoming explicit content in an end-to-end encrypted vault.

Cover works by using an advanced image recognition algorithm to automatically scan your device 24/7 (with your permission of course!) for explicit images & videos. Meaning, as soon as any sensitive photos & videos are detected, Cover will hide that content and/or immediately move it to your passcode-protected private vault. We want you to have the ability to block any content you don’t want to see or want others to see as well as protecting your privacy, allowing you to store personal content on your device. You’ll never need to worry about someone accidentally coming across something they shouldn’t. Keep your content your own. Here’s a list of what Cover has to offer that you won’t find anywhere else:

Auto-Hide™

Cover’s Auto-Hide™ is an innovative image detection AI that automatically scans devices for inappropriate images & videos and secures them in a private vault.

Real-Time Protection™

Real-Time Protection™ means your device will be protected 24/7. Once Cover is installed, it will constantly monitor activity for incoming or hidden sensitive content.

Message Protection

Cover even has your messages protected. Cover will automatically block or “cover” any inappropriate photos or videos sent via Messenger, Whatsapp, Telegram, Wechat, Viber, Line, and more. You’ll also have the option to “uncover” any content that has been blurred but you’d like yo have access to again.

Smart Notifications

If Cover detects sensitive content, you’ll be subtly notified immediately.

Easy to Use

To use Cover, simply install the app from the Google Play Store, grant the app access to store content in your encrypted vault, and let Cover take care of the rest. We are really proud of the fact that the tech used to create Cover means that internet access isn’t even required! Keep your content on your device.
